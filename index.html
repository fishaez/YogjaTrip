Top suggestions

  • Book ferry and flights in advance and save confirmations in one folder/email.
  • For Borobudur, arrive early (sunrise option if you want spectacular views — needs earlier start).
  • Semarang is a long drive — consider train or an overnight if you prefer to rest.
  • Reserve dinners at Bale Raos / House of Raminten on busy nights.

Food picks

  • Singapore — Bugis cafes, local hawker centre dishes (Hainanese chicken rice, laksa).
  • Yogyakarta — Gudeg Sagan, Tempo Gelato, Bakpia Kurnia.
  • Semarang — Lumpia gang Lombok, Nasi Ayam Bu Pini, Toko Oen dessert.

Quick checklist

  • Passport, ferry & flight e-tickets, hotel confirmations.
  • Local currency (IDR) for small purchases & markets.
  • Phone power bank, light rain jacket, comfortable shoes for temple visits.
