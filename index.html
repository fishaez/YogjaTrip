Travel Itinerary: Nov 29 – Dec 5, 2025

Singapore • Batam • Jakarta • Yogyakarta • Semarang

Day 1 – Nov 29 (Sat): Singapore Arrival

Arrival: Singapore Changi Airport

Explore Bugis, Marina Bay, and nearby cafés before taking the evening ferry to Batam.

Day 2 – Nov 30 (Sun): Batam to Jakarta

Morning: Breakfast in Batam

Afternoon: Ferry back to Singapore, then flight to Jakarta (arrive ~12:25pm)

Evening: Dinner near hotel, light sightseeing in Jakarta

Day 3 – Dec 1 (Mon): Jakarta to Yogyakarta

Morning flight to Yogyakarta. Visit Malioboro Street, Kraton Palace, and Taman Sari.

Stay: Central Yogyakarta Airbnb

Day 4 – Dec 2 (Tue): Yogyakarta Temples

Visit Borobudur and Prambanan. Optional visit to local art markets.

Day 5 – Dec 3 (Wed): Yogyakarta to Semarang

Drive to Semarang (approx 3.5–4 hrs). Explore Lawang Sewu and Kota Lama.

Day 6 – Dec 4 (Thu): Semarang

Relax, try local cuisine (Lumpia Semarang, Wingko Babat). Prepare for next-day return.

Day 7 – Dec 5 (Fri): Semarang to Jakarta

Morning: Return drive to airport (CGK flight 13:40–15:00).

End of trip ✈️
